# 1. Website Sitemap + Product Thinking

## Homepage
- **Purpose**: Establish Vitto as an AI-native lending infrastructure platform and show measurable business outcomes.
- **Target audience**: CTO, Chief Digital Officer, Head of Lending, Credit Strategy teams.
- **Conversion goal**: Drive demo requests and platform exploration.

## AI-First Platform Page
- **Purpose**: Explain architecture and module-level technical depth to validate engineering credibility.
- **Target audience**: CTO, Enterprise Architect, Chief Risk Officer.
- **Conversion goal**: Trigger technical evaluation or architecture workshop.

## Lending Lifecycle / Full Stack Automation Page
- **Purpose**: Map platform capabilities to end-to-end lending operations.
- **Target audience**: COO, Head of Operations, LOS/LMS program owners.
- **Conversion goal**: Initiate process-mapping discussion and pilot scope.

## Collections Intelligence Page
- **Purpose**: Demonstrate measurable recovery improvements and operational controls.
- **Target audience**: Head of Collections, Portfolio Risk teams.
- **Conversion goal**: Start collections transformation pilot.

## Agentic AI Page
- **Purpose**: Clarify agent architecture, controls, and safe autonomy in BFSI workflows.
- **Target audience**: CTO, Model Risk team, Compliance stakeholders.
- **Conversion goal**: Approve controlled agent deployment in selected workflows.

## API Infrastructure Page
- **Purpose**: Show integration readiness and security posture.
- **Target audience**: Integration leads, platform engineering teams.
- **Conversion goal**: Begin API sandbox onboarding.

## About / Why Vitto
- **Purpose**: Provide institutional context and product intent.
- **Target audience**: Senior decision makers, procurement teams.
- **Conversion goal**: Build confidence for vendor shortlisting.

## Contact / Request Demo
- **Purpose**: Capture sales-qualified context and deployment needs.
- **Target audience**: Buyer committee representatives.
- **Conversion goal**: Book product + architecture walkthrough.

## Self Sign-Up
- **Purpose**: Enable low-friction entry into sandbox and lead qualification.
- **Target audience**: Product managers, integration engineers, innovation teams.
- **Conversion goal**: Verified lead creation and sandbox activation.
